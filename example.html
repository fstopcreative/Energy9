Simple 4-Step Process

Reclaiming your money shouldn't be complicated. We've streamlined everything for you.

1

Submit Details

2

Record Check

We check whether the record is complete enough for review and may ask for missing documents.

3

Expert Referral

Where suitable, the matter is referred to the approved assessment team or appointed solicitor for further review under their own terms.
